About

Not a portfolio. More like a shelf.

This site is mostly where I dump the stuff that survived long enough to deserve a home. Some of it solves a real problem. Some of it exists because my brain latched onto a dumb idea and refused to shut up.

None of this is trying to be terribly serious. I just like making things, cleaning them up until they feel good, and giving the less embarrassing ones a place to sit.
